Our Hotel

Our beautiful natural hotel Banalata is located at bagmundi, Ajodhya in a beautiful natural forest. Because of its location you can enjoy unlimited natural beauties here.

Apart from excellent room service facility, we provide car and driver accomodation facility so that you can tour Ajodhya without any problem. We provide fantastic camp fire facilities too.

If you truly love nature, you will love to come here again and again.
